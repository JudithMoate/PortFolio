The brain child of "Dinner with chef cola"

Dinner with chef cola was first born in Harare(Zimbabwe)catering for close to 50 people of high status, which then went from 50 to over 70 private dinner functions with chef cola.In all our functions, we have offered the superb services to our cliends with our African style on budget.

FROM HARARE TO CAPE TO THE WORLD!

On the 27th of February 2018, dinner with chef cola was debuted for the first time in South Africa(Cape Town) with private dinner, which was a 3 course meal, a la cart menu at the station on bree, where people from all corners of the world came to network, mingle and enjoy good vegan food on a budget.
